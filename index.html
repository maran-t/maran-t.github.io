Over the last five years I've worked with teams shipping enterprise applications — from invoice and customer management platforms at Trafigura to telecom infrastructure tooling at Tata Communications. Most of my day-to-day is Angular, TypeScript, and the quieter craft of writing code that other engineers will read.
